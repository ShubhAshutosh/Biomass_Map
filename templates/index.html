      How to Use This Web App

      Exploring the Map:

      • Click on a state to zoom in and see its districts.
      • When viewing a state, click on a district to see details about the plants in that district.
      • Hover over a state or district to highlight it.

      Finding the Distance Between Plants:

      1. Click the "Find distance between plants" button on the left.
      2. Start typing the name of the first plant in the top bar. A dropdown list will appear with matching plant names. Select the desired plant.
      3. Do the same for the second plant in the bottom bar.
      4. Click the "OK" button to see the distance between them.
